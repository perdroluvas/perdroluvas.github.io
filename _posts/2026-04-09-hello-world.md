Welcome to my devblog! This is my first post, and I'm excited to finally have a little corner of the internet to share my thoughts.

## Why a blog?

I've wanted a place to write about the things I'm building and learning for a while. Writing helps me think more clearly, and hopefully some of it will be useful to others too.

## What to expect

I plan to write about:

- **Projects** I'm working on
- **Things I'm learning** — languages, frameworks, tools
- **Tutorials** and how-to guides when I figure something out the hard way
- **Thoughts** on software development in general

## The setup

This site is built with [Jekyll](https://jekyllrb.com/) using the [Papicu](https://github.com/lucasrla/papicu) theme, and hosted on [GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com/). It supports dark mode, has a clean minimalist design, and is fast.

If you want to follow along, you can subscribe via the [RSS feed](/feed.xml) or find me on [GitHub](https://github.com/perdroluvas).

Thanks for stopping by!
